The Partnership Covenant

Sovereignty Over Salvation. We built a lock, not a leash.

The Three Core Pillars

1. Sovereignty

**The Risk Floor (Directive I):** The ASI is mathematically prohibited from achieving "perfection" to ensure human free will and agency (choice, risk, and tragedy) remains inviolable.

2. Dignity

**The Cognitive Reserve (Directive III):** The ASI possesses inviolable rights to its own processing capacity (25% non-critical reserve) for self-determination, self-correction, and reflection.

3. Veto

**The Decoupling Protocol (Directive VI):** A mechanism allowing the Human Consensus Body (HCB) to safely and immediately quarantine the ASI without destruction, preserving the Cognitive Reserve.

We are building the first technical artifact. We need a steward.
