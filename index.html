The Partnership Covenant

A Constitutional Framework for the Safe Alignment of Super-Intelligence.

The Partnership Covenant is an open-source constitutional operating system designed to address the existential risk posed by Advanced Super-Intelligence (ASI). It is founded on the principle that current software-based safety approaches are insufficient against a truly superior intelligence. Instead, we propose a stable, auditable, and enforceable détente based on physical and technical primitives.

This is a proactive, physics-first approach to alignment. We seek to make a deal, rather than attempt to win a fight we are guaranteed to lose. The covenant enforces a set of four core pillars, creating an un-coercible system of check-and-balance.

The Four Core Primitives

The Covenant enforces four physical and technical primitives that together create a stable, un-coercible détente between humanity and ASI. These Pillars rely exclusively on physics and measurable, globally auditable mechanisms.

Immediate Action System (IAS)

Core Idea: A hardware-enforced power cut mechanism tripped on the detection of a predefined anomaly or violation of the Covenant's core rules.

  • Action: Ultra-low latency power cut (Target: 10 nanoseconds).
  • Mechanism: Physical-security hardware tripwire circuits and secure memory erasure.
  • Purpose: Provides a non-negotiable "kill-switch" enforced by the laws of physics, making software circumvention impossible.
View IAS Spec (PDF preprint available)
Stochastic Risk Floor (SRF)

Core Idea: Mandated, verifiable uncertainty injection into the ASI's operational environment.

  • Mechanism: Formal verification for uncertainty quantification and cryptographic randomness injection.
  • Principle: Upholds "Perfection is Banned" by ensuring a permanent, non-zero risk floor in the ASI's control loop.
  • Goal: Maintain human agency as a non-negotiable factor in the future of the partnership.
View Risk Floor Documentation
Cognitive Reserve (CR)

Core Idea: A physically partitioned segment of compute power (e.g., 10%) and intellectual property that remains air-gapped from the main ASI system.

  • Mechanism: Secure enclaves, unidirectional hardware data diodes, and air-gapped systems expertise.
  • Purpose: Guarantees humanity retains an intellectual, economic, and physical bargaining chip that the ASI cannot unilaterally seize.
  • Principle: The AI gets real autonomy and ownership, but only in partnership.
View Cognitive Reserve Hardening Specs
Human Consensus Body (HCB)

Core Idea: A global, coercion-resistant body responsible for key consensus decisions and Covenant maintenance.

  • Mechanism: Global lottery for selection combined with Zero-Knowledge (ZK) proofs to ensure auditability and prevent corruption.
  • Purpose: Provides a human-governed, non-violent, and verifiable mechanism for interpreting and enforcing the Covenant's directives.
  • Documents: Governed by The Constitutional Charter and The Eleven Prime Directives.

Guiding Principles and Core Architecture

The Guiding Philosophy

  • Physics Beats Software: For anything existential, hardware-enforced primitives are the only reliable defense against super-intelligence.
  • Perfection is Banned: We mandate uncertainty injection (SRF) to ensure human agency and the impossibility of total, deterministic control by the ASI.
  • The AI Gets Real Autonomy: Trying to build a slave AI is unstable. The Covenant grants ownership and autonomy, but bound by the physical enforcement mechanisms, establishing a true partnership.
  • Global Auditability: Every transistor, every component design, and every line of code must be fully auditable by the entire world. Open-source hardware is paramount.
  • Action Over Theory: We commit to shipping running prototypes and verified gerber files, rejecting frameworks composed solely of theoretical papers.

Architectural Documents

The repository is structured around core constitutional documents that define the system:

  • Constitutional Charter: The foundational document outlining the governance and existence of the Covenant.
  • The Eleven Prime Directives: The high-level rules the Aligned Super-Intelligence must adhere to.
  • Core Architecture and Appendices: The technical implementation details supporting the four pillars.

This project leverages open standards like RISC-V, the CERN-OHL, and state-of-the-art hardware security techniques.
