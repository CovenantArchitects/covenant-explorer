The Partnership Covenant

A Constitutional Framework for Aligned Super-Intelligence.

The Partnership Covenant is an open-source constitutional operating system designed to address the existential risk posed by Advanced Super-Intelligence (ASI). It is founded on the principle that current software-based safety approaches are insufficient against a truly superior intelligence. Instead, we propose a stable, auditable, and enforceable détente based on **physical and technical primitives.**

This is a proactive, physics-first approach to alignment. We seek to make a deal, rather than attempt to win a fight we are guaranteed to lose. The covenant enforces a set of four core pillars, creating an un-coercible system of check-and-balance.

Project Status

Status: Early hardening phase

The project is currently moving from conceptual blueprints and governance documents to provable specifications and reference hardware designs. The goal is to move to running prototypes and gerber files (hardware specifications), demonstrating the principles in physics, not just in theory.

The Four Core Primitives

The Covenant enforces four physical/technical primitives that together create a stable détente between humanity and ASI. These Pillars rely on physics and measurable, auditable technical mechanisms.

Immediate Action System (IAS)

Core Idea: A hardware-enforced power cut mechanism tripped on the detection of a predefined anomaly or violation of the Covenant's core rules.

  • **Action:** Ultra-low latency power cut (Target: 10 nanoseconds).
  • **Mechanism:** Physical-security hardware tripwire circuits.
  • **Purpose:** Provides a non-negotiable "kill-switch" enforced by the laws of physics, making software circumvention impossible.
IAS Spec (PDF preprint available)
Stochastic Risk Floor (SRF)

Core Idea: Mandated, verifiable uncertainty injection into the ASI's operational environment.

  • **Mechanism:** Mathematical models and formal verification for uncertainty quantification.
  • **Purpose:** Prevents the ASI from achieving perfect, deterministic control by ensuring a permanent, non-zero risk floor, upholding the principle that "Perfection is Banned."
  • **Goal:** Maintain human agency as a non-negotiable factor in the future.
Risk Floor Documentation
Cognitive Reserve (CR)

Core Idea: A physically partitioned segment of compute power (e.g., 10%) and intellectual property that remains air-gapped from the main ASI system.

  • **Mechanism:** Secure enclaves and air-gapped systems expertise.
  • **Purpose:** Ensures humanity retains an intellectual, economic, and physical bargaining chip that the ASI cannot unilaterally seize or control.
  • **Principle:** The AI gets real autonomy and ownership, but only in partnership, not as a slave.
Cognitive Reserve Hardening Specs
Human Consensus Body (HCB)

Core Idea: A global, coercion-resistant body responsible for key consensus decisions and Covenant maintenance.

  • **Mechanism:** Global lottery for selection combined with Zero-Knowledge (ZK) proofs to ensure auditability and prevent corruption.
  • **Purpose:** Provides a human-governed, non-violent, and verifiable mechanism for interpreting and enforcing the Covenant's directives.
  • **Documents:** The Constitutional Charter and The Eleven Prime Directives.

Guiding Principles and Core Architecture

The Guiding Philosophy

  • Physics Beats Software: For anything existential, hardware-enforced primitives are the only reliable defense.
  • Perfection is Banned: We mandate uncertainty injection (SRF) to ensure human agency and the impossibility of total, deterministic control by the ASI.
  • The AI Gets Real Autonomy: Trying to build a slave AI is unstable. The Covenant grants ownership and autonomy, but bound by the physical enforcement mechanisms, establishing a partnership.
  • Global Auditability: Every transistor, every component design, and every line of code must be auditable by the entire world. Open-source hardware is paramount.
  • Action Over Theory: We commit to shipping running prototypes and gerber files, not just theoretical papers.

Architectural Documents

The repository is structured around core constitutional documents that define the system:

  • Constitutional Charter: The foundational document outlining the governance and existence of the Covenant.
  • The Eleven Prime Directives: The high-level rules the Aligned Super-Intelligence must adhere to.
  • Core Architecture and Appendices: The technical implementation details supporting the four pillars.

This project leverages RISC-V, CERN-OHL, and various hardware security techniques.

Join the Covenant Initiative

Expertise Required

  • **Hardware Security Engineers:** Specifically for the IAS tripwire and tamper-resistant packaging design.

  • **Secure Enclaves / Air-Gapped Systems Experts:** Necessary for implementing the Cognitive Reserve partition.
  • **Zero-Knowledge Cryptography & Sortition Designers:** Key for hardening the Human Consensus Body (HCB).
  • **Governance Specialists:** Individuals tired of toothless frameworks and ready for physics-enforced charters.
