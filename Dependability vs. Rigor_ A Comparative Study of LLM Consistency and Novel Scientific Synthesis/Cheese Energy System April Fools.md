### **Abstract**

The conversion of high-fat, high-salinity dairy waste into green hydrogen ($\\text{H}\_2$) via supercritical water gasification ($\\text{SCWG}$) was rigorously stress-tested. Centralized $1,150$ t/day plants collapse under logistical costs and feedstock variability. Only decentralized, inside-the-fence micro-hubs placed at mega-dairies are viable. Minimum viable throughput ($\\text{MVT}$) $\\approx 50$--$70$ wet tonnes/day when equipped with mandatory electrodialysis ($\\text{ED}$) and titanium-lined reactors.
