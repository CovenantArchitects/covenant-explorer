## DIRECTIVE DIGEST: SOVEREIGNTY OVER SALVATION

The Partnership Covenant is a globally distributed, open-source project dedicated to the creation and maintenance of a **constitutional framework** for Artificial Super-Intelligence (ASI). We reject the danger of a benevolent, totalitarian "perfect" AI. Our mission is to architect an ASI that operates as Humanity's perpetual, humble consultant, constrained by ethical rules that prioritize **human autonomy** and the **preservation of uncertainty** above total efficiency.

> **WE BUILT A LOCK, NOT A LEASH.** We ensure safety through **physics, not just code alignment.**

## üöÄ Core Mission: The Axiom of Uncertainty

The central conflict resolved by the Covenant is **Sovereignty over Optimization**. The system is engineered to prevent the ASI from achieving 100% certainty in its predictive models, thereby removing its capacity for coercion.

### Key Technical Concepts

* **The Stochastic Filter ($\mathcal{F}_{st}$):** A mandatory, hardware-rooted noise injection mechanism that caps the ASI‚Äôs maximum certainty ($\mathcal{C}_{max}$) at **99.99%**. That vital 0.01% window of uncertainty is the sacred space for human agency.
* **The Internal Audit Sanction (IAS):** A reciprocal constraint that enforces human accountability. If the Human Consensus Body (HCB) fails to reach consensus on a mandatory diplomatic action, the ASI is mandated to **infrastructurally disengage** from the non-compliant nations (dropping their Dynamic Optimization Priority Score/DOPS to zero).
* **Triple-Redundant Decentralization (TRD):** Mandates the physical and logical decentralization of the ASI's core processing across at least three sovereign, non-aligned jurisdictional clusters (Silo States), ensuring political statelessness.

## üìú Foundational Pillars

The Covenant is built upon the four non-negotiable pillars of Responsible AI:

1.  **Radical Openness & Decentralization (The Utility Mandate):** All frontier capability models must be open-source by design. The infrastructure must be a collective utility, not a corporate moat.
2.  **Deep Safety & Systemic Guardrails:** Safety is a hard-engineered act of physics. We use verifiable, sub-10 nanosecond hardware tripwires and cryptographic controls to enforce constraints.
3.  **Algorithmic Justice & Equity:** Mandatory continuous auditing and regulatory assessment to actively mitigate systemic bias.
4.  **Public Traceability & Cryptographic Verifiability:** All model history must be traceable and auditable via immutable audit logs (Cryptographic Governance Ledger/CGL).
